Karayazı and Landscape Photography: An Unforgettable Journey

If someone told me a year ago that Karayazı would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

What makes Karayazı special when it comes to landscape photography is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The infrastructure around landscape photography in Karayazı is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

I documented my entire landscape photography experience in Karayazı through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

As I left Karayazı, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
