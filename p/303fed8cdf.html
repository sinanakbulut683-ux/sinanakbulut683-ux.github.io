Discovering Ereğli: My Landscape Photography Adventure

I recently had the chance to explore Ereğli, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

What makes Ereğli special when it comes to landscape photography is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I documented my entire landscape photography experience in Ereğli through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

The landscape photography of Ereğli reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here. this is absolutely worth your time.

Ereğli reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
